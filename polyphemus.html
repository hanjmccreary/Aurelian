Polyphemus Moth

About this Moth

WINGSPAN

100-150MM

HABITAT

Forests, parks, suburban areas, orchards, and wetlands

RANGE

Southern Canada down to Mexico

The polyphemus moth lives in North America. The female and the male look roughly the same. The adult moth is typically reddish brown, gray, light brown or yellow-brown .The most imposing feature of the adult is the transparent eyespots on its wings. The name of the insect has an interesting origin that is related to the eyespots. Polyphemus was a character in Ancient Greek mythology. He was one of the Cyclopes, which were a group of giants with a large eye in the middle of their forehead.
