Cecropia Moth

About this Moth

WINGSPAN

130-180 MM

HABITAT

Maple, birch and cherry trees in moist areas east of the Rocky Mountains

RANGE

East of the Rocky Mountains in the United States and Canada

Cecropia moths live in North America and are nocturnal. They have a wingspan of five to seven inches and are the largest moths on the continent. They are attractive animals whose wings display red, brown, black, and white markings in an interesting design. The front of their body has white and red or orange hair, and their abdomen is banded.
